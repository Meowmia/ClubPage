BICEP LIVE x PRINTWORKS – REVIEW

If, like us, you’ve been hammering Bicep’s debut album since its release last year then it’s highly likely that you’ve been dying to catch them perform live. That’s not even taking into consideration their hefty back catalogue that spans almost a decade with monster releases such as Just, Vision of Love and collaborations with the likes of Simian Mobile Disco and Midland. All taken into consideration, we quickly snapped up tickets when it was announced that Bicep would be playing at London’s Printworks in November.

HOSH & 1979 FEAT JALJA – MIDNIGHT (THE BLOCKCHAIN REMIX)

This remix of HOSH & 1979’s Midnight from The Blockchain will have you drawn in from the start. The rolling groove, playful vocals and hypnotic synths all come together to create one mighty crowd pleaser. Combined with an intense and euphoric drop courtesy of an arpeggiated synth and expertly cut vocals, the release continues to build until you realise your whole body is pumping. What makes this release even sweeter is that this it is available as a FREE download from Dear Deer Music, a Kyiv based record label. Head on over to their Soundcloud to get your hands on it.

ELLEN ALLIEN LAUNCHES NEW RECORD LABEL – UFO INC

Ellen Allien has just marked the launch of her brand new UFO Inc. label with a hard hitting, two track EP. Featuring the tracks UFO, which has been on heavy rotation in her DJ sets throughout the second half of 2018, and hard-hitting acid belter Körpermaschine on the B-Side, it’s a solid introduction to the raw and contemporary techno releases we might expect from the imprint. ‘UFO serves a mediator between earth and heaven, i.e. the concrete dancefloor and a feeling of transcendental rapture – the kind of track that lifts you up from where you dance and takes you away to new spheres’ – Ellen Allien The title track is aptly named, with hypnotic synths and acid stabs, UFO is an extraterrestrial sonic journey that certainly fits with Allien’s vision for the label. Körpermaschine is a raw, industrial track with punishing percussion and and an unforgiving acid bass-line that sets the tone for the harder and more brutal releases to come on the label.
